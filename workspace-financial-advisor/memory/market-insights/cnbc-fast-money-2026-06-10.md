# CNBC Fast Money — June 9, 2026 (Digested June 10)

**Episode:** A Decline in Chip Stocks Becomes Market Sell-off… Where Tech Sector is Heading
**Duration:** 44m

## Summary

Host Melissa Lee and the Fast Money roundtable covered a wild session where chip stocks reversed sharply amid a broader market sell-off triggered by geopolitical escalation between the U.S. and Iran. Featured interview with Kalshi CEO Tarek Mansour on the launch of regulated perpetual futures.

## Key Topics

1. **Chip stock reversal** — SMH (semiconductor ETF) dropped 1% after a 6% bounce Monday; the sector had its worst day in six years on Friday
2. **Geopolitical catalyst** — Iran shot down a U.S. helicopter; Trump threatened resumed kinetic strikes; SOXX swung from +3% to -8.6% intraday
3. **Kalshi perps** — Kalshi hit $1B in perpetual futures volume within one week of launch; first regulated perp contracts in the U.S.
4. **Apple sell-off** — AAPL tumbling after disappointing WWDC; Siri AI announcements failed to impress investors
5. **Defensive rotation** — 5th consecutive session of rotation out of mega-cap tech into consumer staples, healthcare, utilities
6. **Market snapshot** — S&P 500 -0.26%, Nasdaq -0.97%, Dow +0.17%; VIX elevated at ~20-22; 10Y yield ~4.56%
