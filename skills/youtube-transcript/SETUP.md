## Quick Setup (2 minutes)

1. **Go to RapidAPI:**
   - Visit: https://rapidapi.com/hub
   - Log in with your account

2. **Subscribe to a Transcript API:**
   
   **Option A (Recommended):**
   - Search for "YouTube Transcriptor" by Glavier
   - URL: https://rapidapi.com/Glavier/api/youtube-transcriptor
   - Subscribe to the **Free tier** (500 requests/month)

   **Option B:**
   - Search for "YouTube Transcript"
   - Pick any with good reviews and a free tier
   - Subscribe

3. **Test it:**
   - Come back here and share any YouTube URL
   - I'll fetch the transcript and summarize it
